Recommended GSS 1972-2024 Release 3 CSV subset variables for EHI v0.4.5:

year
age
marital
marcohab
hapmar
hapcohab
xmoviey
sexfreq
xmarsex
wtssps

Minimum required by the extractor:
year, hapmar, hapcohab, xmoviey, sexfreq, xmarsex, wtssps

age, marital, and marcohab are recommended because they enable the diagnostic
SEXFREQ stratifications. The extractor accepts numeric-coded or labelled CSV.
